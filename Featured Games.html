Hell's Contours

Hell's Contours is an activity experience game played from either a third-individual or first-individual point of view. Players complete missions—direct situations with set targets—to advance through the story. Outside of the missions, players may uninhibitedly meander the open world.

Rain Fall Thunderdome

Rain Fall:Thunderdome is an activity experience endurance game played from a first or third individual point of view that permits players to take part in four head exercises: investigation, endurance, battle, and exchanging.
